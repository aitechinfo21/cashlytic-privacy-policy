Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Cashlytic ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal and financial information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We collect information that you directly provide when using Cashlytic:

  • Financial Information: Expense transactions, budget amounts, categories, payment methods, and related financial data you enter into the App
  • Account Information: Settings, preferences, and configuration data you choose to save
  • Backup Data: Information you choose to export or backup from the App

1.2 Information Automatically Collected

The App may automatically collect certain information to provide core functionality:

  • Device Information: Device identifiers, operating system version, and device model (used for notifications and app functionality)
  • App Usage Data: Feature usage, interaction patterns, and app performance metrics (collected locally only)
  • SMS Messages: If you grant SMS permission, the App may access SMS messages to automatically detect and parse transaction notifications from banks and financial institutions. Only transaction-related SMS messages are processed, and this data is used solely for expense tracking within the App.

1.3 Permissions We Request

Cashlytic may request the following permissions to provide specific features:

  • Notifications: To send you reminders, budget alerts, and app notifications
  • Biometric Authentication: To secure your app with fingerprint or face recognition (optional feature)
  • SMS Access: To automatically detect and import transaction information from bank SMS messages (optional feature)
  • Camera: To scan receipts and extract expense information using OCR (optional feature)
  • Microphone: To enable voice-based expense logging (optional feature)
  • Storage/File Access: To export and import backup files

Important: All permissions are optional. You can use Cashlytic without granting any of these permissions, though some features may be limited. You can revoke permissions at any time through your device settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely for the following purposes:

  • Core Functionality: To provide expense tracking, budget management, financial analysis, and reporting features
  • Automation: To automatically detect and import transactions from SMS messages (if permission granted)
  • Notifications: To send you budget alerts, reminders, and app-related notifications
  • App Security: To implement biometric authentication and app locking features
  • Data Backup: To enable you to export and restore your data
  • App Improvement: To understand app usage patterns and improve user experience (data analyzed locally only)

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local-First Architecture

Cashlytic uses a local-first approach to data storage. This means:

  • All your financial data is stored locally on your device using encrypted local databases
  • Your data is never automatically transmitted to external servers or cloud services
  • We use industry-standard encryption (AES-256) to protect sensitive data stored on your device
  • No financial information is sent to third-party analytics or tracking services

3.2 Security Measures

We implement the following security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: Sensitive data is encrypted at rest using secure storage mechanisms
  • App Lock: Optional biometric authentication and PIN protection to prevent unauthorized access
  • No Cloud Sync: By default, your data remains on your device and is not synced to cloud services
  • Secure Backup: If you choose to export data, it is stored in files you control on your device or shared storage

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 No Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal or financial information with third parties. Your data remains private and under your control.

4.2 Limited Exceptions

We may disclose your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Your Consent: If you explicitly choose to share data through export or backup features
  • Service Providers: We do not currently use third-party service providers that process your financial data. If this changes in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy and obtain your consent where required.

4.3 No Analytics or Advertising

Cashlytic does not currently integrate with:

  • Third-party analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, etc.)
  • Advertising networks or ad SDKs
  • Crash reporting services that transmit personal data
  • Social media platforms

If we add any of these services in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy and provide you with opt-out options where applicable.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can view all your data directly within the App
  • Modification: You can edit, update, or delete any expense, budget, or other data at any time
  • Export: You can export your data to a file on your device at any time
  • Deletion: You can delete all app data by uninstalling the App or using the app's data deletion features
  • Permission Control: You can grant or revoke app permissions at any time through your device settings
  • Opt-Out: You can disable optional features (SMS import, notifications, etc.) at any time

6. Children's Privacy

Cashlytic is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

7. Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device for as long as you use the App. When you delete data within the App or uninstall the App, the data is removed from your device. We do not retain copies of your data on external servers.

If you export your data to a backup file, that file is stored on your device or shared storage under your control, and you are responsible for managing its retention and deletion.

8. International Data Transfers

Since Cashlytic uses a local-first architecture and does not transmit your data to external servers, there are no international data transfers of your personal or financial information. All data processing occurs locally on your device.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy
  • Posting a notice in the App (for significant changes)
  • Providing other notification methods as appropriate

Your continued use of the App after any changes to this Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
